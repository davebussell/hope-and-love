Therapy & Counselling in Prince Edward County

In-person and virtual psychotherapy in the County — Picton, Wellington, Bloomfield, and beyond — with Matt Harris, Registered Psychotherapist. Support for anxiety, depression, relationships, grief, and addiction.

Picton · Wellington · Bloomfield In-Person & Virtual Sessions Registered Psychotherapist

Prince Edward County is a place people love, and also a place where support can be genuinely hard to reach. The bridge-and-ferry geography, a seasonal economy that swings from a summer rush to a quiet winter, and limited local services mean that mental health care is not always easy to find when you need it. Hope & Love offers therapy right in the County, in person or by secure video.

The rhythm of County life shapes what people bring to therapy: the pressure of the tourist season, the isolation that can settle in over winter, the strain on hospitality and small-business owners, and the quiet loneliness that a beautiful rural place can hide.

Counselling services in Prince Edward County

  • Anxiety therapy in the County — worry, panic, and seasonal pressure
  • Depression therapy in the County — low mood, winter isolation, and disconnection
  • Couples therapy in the County — communication, trust, and reconnection
  • Addiction therapy in the County — substance use and behavioural addiction
  • Grief counselling, trauma therapy, and burnout support

In-person therapy in the County

Prince Edward County is one of Hope & Love’s two in-person locations, alongside Belleville. If you would rather not drive off-island for support, in-person sessions in the County can be arranged during your free consultation.

Virtual therapy, especially through the winter

Secure video sessions are available year-round to anyone in Picton, Wellington, Bloomfield, Consecon, Cherry Valley, Milford, and the surrounding communities. For many County residents, virtual therapy is the most practical option once the season turns — no winter drive, no ferry timing, and consistent support even when local services wind down.

Who Hope & Love works with in the County

  • Year-round residents who want local support without leaving the County
  • Hospitality and small-business owners carrying the weight of a seasonal economy
  • Retirees, newcomers, and second-home owners navigating change or isolation
  • Couples and individuals wanting steady support through the quieter months

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer in-person sessions in Prince Edward County?
Yes. The County is one of Hope & Love’s two in-person locations, along with Belleville. In-person appointments can be arranged during your consultation, and virtual sessions are available year-round across the County and Ontario.
Is therapy available in the County through the winter?
Yes. Secure video sessions continue year-round, which many residents find more practical once the season winds down and driving conditions change. Support stays consistent regardless of the time of year.
Which County communities do you serve?
Picton, Wellington, Bloomfield, Consecon, Cherry Valley, Milford, Ameliasburgh, and surrounding communities. Virtual sessions are available to anyone in the County and across Ontario.
Does insurance cover therapy in the County?
Many Ontario extended health benefit plans cover sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist. Matt provides detailed receipts for insurance submission. Check your plan for "psychotherapy" or "registered psychotherapist" coverage.
