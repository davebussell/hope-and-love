About Hope & Love
Whatever you are carrying - anxiety that will not quiet, depression that will not lift, addiction that will not let go - you do not have to keep carrying it alone.

People come to therapy for all kinds of reasons. Sometimes it is a crisis. Sometimes it is a pattern that has been there for years and finally feels too heavy. Sometimes it is a relationship that is fracturing, or a version of yourself you no longer recognize.

Hope & Love is a private psychotherapy practice in Ontario offering individual and couples therapy across a broad range of challenges - anxiety, depression, addiction, trauma, grief, and the relationship difficulties that so often come alongside them.

The work is collaborative, honest, and grounded in what matters to you. There is no pressure toward a particular outcome, no judgment about where you are starting from, and no pace you will be pushed to move faster than you are ready for.

Your Therapist

Matt Harris

Registered Psychotherapist • Ontario

Matt brings warmth, clinical expertise, and lived experience to every session. He works with individuals and couples navigating anxiety, depression, addiction, and relationship challenges, drawing on evidence-based approaches that are both rigorous and genuinely compassionate.

His approach is collaborative, honest, and person-centred. He does not push you toward an outcome. He offers a grounded, practical path forward at a pace that is yours.

How We Work

Therapy that meets you where you are

Step 01

Free 20-minute consultation

No paperwork, no commitment. We get to know each other and make sure working together is the right fit before anything else.

Step 02

Personalized support plan

Your story is your own. Together we build a clear, collaborative plan grounded in your goals, your safety, and your lived experience.

Step 03

Ongoing sessions, practical progress

Weekly or bi-weekly sessions, in-person or secure video, with consistent support and honest accountability toward the life you want.
