Insurance & Payment

Using your extended health benefits

Many extended health benefit plans in Ontario cover sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist (RP). Coverage varies by plan, but psychotherapy is increasingly included as a covered benefit. If you are unsure whether your plan covers therapy, check your benefits booklet or call your provider and ask specifically about coverage for a Registered Psychotherapist.

What Matt provides

After each session, Matt provides a detailed receipt that includes his name, registration number (CRPO), session date, session type, and fee paid. This is typically all that is required for insurance reimbursement. You pay at the time of the session and submit the receipt to your insurer directly.

Payment methods

Payment is accepted by e-transfer, credit card, or debit. Payment is due at the time of each session.

Cancellation policy

Hope & Love asks for at least 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or missed appointments without notice may be charged the full session fee. If you are unwell or facing an emergency, please reach out, we will work with you.

A note on sliding scale

A limited number of reduced-fee spots may be available for individuals facing genuine financial hardship. If fees are a barrier to accessing support, please mention this when you reach out and we will do our best to find a workable arrangement.

Common Questions

Fees & billing questions

Does my insurance cover therapy with Matt?
Many extended health plans cover Registered Psychotherapists. Check your benefits for "psychotherapy" or "registered psychotherapist." Matt provides detailed receipts for direct submission to your insurer.
Do I pay per session or in advance?
You pay per session, at the time of the session. There are no packages or prepayment requirements. Sessions can be booked individually and you are free to pause or stop at any time.
Is the free consultation really free?
Yes. The initial 20-minute consultation is completely free and carries no obligation. It is a chance for you to ask questions, share a little about what you are looking for, and decide whether working together feels like the right fit.
What if I need to cancel a session?
We ask for at least 48 hours notice. Late cancellations or missed appointments without notice may be charged the full session fee. Please reach out if something comes up, we are always willing to work with difficult circumstances.
Is couples therapy covered by insurance?
Some plans cover couples therapy when billed by a Registered Psychotherapist. Coverage varies significantly between plans. Check your benefits specifically for "couples therapy" or "marital therapy" under your psychotherapy benefit.
Can I claim therapy on my taxes?
In some cases, therapy with a Registered Psychotherapist may be claimable as a medical expense in Canada. We recommend confirming with your accountant or the CRA. Matt provides receipts that include all the information needed for tax purposes.

Clinical responsibility: Hope & Love provides therapy, counselling, and support. Our services are not a substitute for emergency care or crisis intervention. If you or someone else is in immediate danger, contact emergency services or a local crisis resource.
