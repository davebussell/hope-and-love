Frequently asked questions

Do you have an office in Kingston?
No. All sessions for Kingston clients are held online by secure video. If you prefer in-person therapy, sessions are available in Belleville and Prince Edward County. Virtual sessions are available to anyone in Ontario.
Does insurance cover addiction therapy in Kingston?
Many Ontario extended health benefit plans cover sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist. Matt provides detailed receipts for insurance submission. Check your plan for "psychotherapy" or "registered psychotherapist" coverage.
Can couples attend therapy together in Kingston?
Yes. Virtual couples therapy is available to clients in Kingston and the surrounding Limestone Region.
