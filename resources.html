What Therapy Actually Does for People Struggling with Addiction

Crisis & Emergency Resources

If you need immediate support

Hope & Love is not a crisis or emergency service. The following resources provide immediate support across Ontario:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, call or text 988 (available across Canada)
  • Ontario Problem Gambling Helpline, 1-888-230-3505 (24/7, free, confidential)
  • ConnexOntario Mental Health Helpline, 1-866-531-2600
  • Emergency, call 911
