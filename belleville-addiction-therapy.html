Frequently asked questions

Is in-person addiction therapy available in Belleville?
Yes. In-person sessions are available in Belleville and the Quinte region. Contact us to confirm current availability. Virtual sessions are available for anyone who prefers them.
Does insurance cover addiction therapy in Belleville?
Many Ontario extended health benefit plans cover sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist. Matt provides detailed receipts for insurance submission. Check your plan for "psychotherapy" or "registered psychotherapist" coverage.
Is virtual therapy available in smaller communities near Belleville?
Yes. Virtual therapy is available to anyone in Ontario, including Trenton, Picton, Napanee, and surrounding communities.
