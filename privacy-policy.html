Privacy & Cookie Policy

How Hope & Love Counselling handles your information on this website, and how you can control cookies. Last updated: July 2026.

This policy explains what information the Hope & Love Counselling website (hopeandlove.ca) collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. It applies to this website only.

Your therapy is confidential and separate from this website

The privacy of your therapy sessions is protected by professional and legal confidentiality standards for Registered Psychotherapists in Ontario, and is handled entirely outside this website. Booking and clinical records are managed through our practice-management platform (Jane), not through this site. This policy covers only the information handled by the website itself.

Information we collect

  • Contact form. If you send a message through our contact form, we receive the name, email, and details you provide so we can respond. Form submissions are processed by our website host, Netlify.
  • Booking. When you book a consultation, you are taken to our secure booking platform (Jane / JaneApp), which handles that information under its own privacy terms.
  • Analytics & advertising. With your consent, we use Google Analytics and Google Ads to understand how the site is used and to measure our advertising. This may include your approximate location, device and browser type, and the pages you visit. We do not use this information to identify you personally.

Cookies we use

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We group them into three categories:

  • Strictly necessary — required for the site to function and to remember your cookie choice. These are always active.
  • Analytics — Google Analytics (GA4), used only with your consent, to understand how visitors use the site.
  • Marketing — Google Ads, used only with your consent, to measure and improve our advertising.

When you first visit, analytics and marketing cookies stay off until you choose to accept them. You can change your choice at any time:

Third-party services

Some information is handled by trusted third parties, each under their own privacy policies: Google (analytics and advertising), Netlify (website hosting and contact-form delivery), and Jane (booking and practice management). We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

  • Accept, decline, or customise cookies using the banner or the button above.
  • Adjust or block cookies through your browser settings at any time.
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of information you have sent us, by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information? Email matt@hopeandlove.ca or use our contact page.

This page is provided for general information and is not legal advice.
