Therapy & Counselling in Belleville

In-person and virtual psychotherapy in Belleville and across the Bay of Quinte, with Matt Harris, Registered Psychotherapist. Support for anxiety, depression, relationships, grief, and addiction.

Belleville & Quinte Region In-Person & Virtual Sessions Registered Psychotherapist

Belleville is the heart of the Bay of Quinte, and for a city its size, genuinely specialized private therapy can be hard to find. Many people end up on long waitlists or travelling to Kingston. Hope & Love offers a focused, relationship-based practice right here, with sessions available in person in Belleville or by secure video anywhere in Ontario.

Whether you are a professional downtown, a parent juggling family life, a Loyalist College student, or someone who has simply been carrying something heavy for a long time, therapy here begins with understanding your situation, not fitting you into a program.

Counselling services in Belleville

  • Anxiety therapy in Belleville — worry, panic, social anxiety, and health anxiety
  • Depression therapy in Belleville — low mood, burnout, and seasonal depression
  • Couples therapy in Belleville — communication, trust, and reconnection
  • Grief counselling in Belleville & Quinte — bereavement and loss
  • Addiction therapy in Belleville — substance use and behavioural addiction
  • Trauma & PTSD therapy and anger management

In-person therapy in Belleville

Belleville is one of Hope & Love’s two in-person locations, alongside Prince Edward County. If meeting face to face matters to you, in-person appointments are available and can be arranged during your free consultation. It is a genuine local option, not a virtual-only service with a Belleville address.

Virtual therapy across the Quinte region

Secure video sessions are available to anyone in the region, including Quinte West and Trenton, Napanee, Stirling, Frankford, Deseronto, and the smaller communities around the bay. For many people in a close-knit area, virtual sessions also offer a welcome layer of privacy: no waiting room, no familiar faces.

Who Hope & Love works with in Belleville

  • Individuals facing anxiety, depression, grief, or a pattern that has become hard to carry alone
  • Couples wanting to rebuild communication, trust, or connection
  • People navigating addiction, and the partners and families affected by it
  • Anyone who has been on a waitlist and wants to start sooner

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you have an office in Belleville?
Yes. Belleville is one of Hope & Love’s two in-person locations, along with Prince Edward County. In-person appointments can be arranged during your consultation, and virtual sessions are available to anyone in the Quinte region and across Ontario.
What kinds of therapy do you offer in Belleville?
Individual therapy for anxiety, depression, grief, trauma, and addiction, as well as couples therapy. Matt is a Registered Psychotherapist drawing on evidence-based approaches including CBT, EFT, IFS, DBT, and EMDR, matched to what is useful for you.
Does insurance cover therapy in Belleville?
Many Ontario extended health benefit plans cover sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist. Matt provides detailed receipts for insurance submission. Check your plan for "psychotherapy" or "registered psychotherapist" coverage.
Which communities near Belleville do you serve?
Virtual therapy is available throughout the Quinte region and Ontario, including Trenton, Quinte West, Napanee, Stirling, Frankford, Deseronto, and Prince Edward County. In-person sessions are offered in Belleville and the County.

Hope & Love is not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger, call 911. For mental health crisis support, call or text 988.
