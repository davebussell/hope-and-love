Addiction Therapy in Prince Edward County

Specialized addiction counselling for individuals and couples in Prince Edward County — in-person and virtual sessions with Matt Harris, Registered Psychotherapist.

Prince Edward County In-person available Virtual option province-wide Registered Psychotherapist

Prince Edward County is a community of strong values, quiet beauty, and real people navigating real struggles. Hope & Love offers specialized addiction therapy for individuals and couples in the County — combining clinical rigour with compassionate, person-centred support. Sessions are available virtually and, where possible, in person.

Addiction therapy services available in Prince Edward County

  • Pornography addiction therapy — including faith-informed approaches and couples work
  • Sex addiction and compulsive sexual behaviour therapy
  • Gambling addiction therapy
  • Drug addiction therapy
  • Alcohol use disorder counselling
  • Technology and screen addiction therapy
  • Couples therapy for addiction and betrayal trauma
  • Partner support for spouses and family members affected by addiction
  • Shame and self-worth counselling
  • Faith-informed addiction therapy

Virtual therapy for Prince Edward County clients

Virtual therapy is available province-wide and is the primary access point for most clients in the County. Secure video sessions offer full confidentiality, flexible scheduling, and access to specialized therapy without the travel of going to a larger centre.

Who Hope & Love serves in Prince Edward County

  • Individuals struggling with compulsive patterns they have not been able to address alone
  • Couples navigating the aftermath of disclosure, deception, or betrayal
  • Partners and family members of people with addiction
  • People seeking faith-integrated counselling in a professional therapeutic context

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is addiction therapy available in Prince Edward County?
Yes. Virtual sessions are available to anyone in the County. In-person availability can be discussed during your consultation. Many County clients find virtual therapy more practical and accessible.
Does insurance cover addiction therapy?
Many Ontario extended health benefit plans cover sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist. Matt provides detailed receipts for insurance submission. Check your plan for "psychotherapy" or "registered psychotherapist" coverage.
Can couples attend therapy from Prince Edward County?
Yes. Virtual couples therapy is available to couples in Prince Edward County and can accommodate partners in different locations across Ontario.
