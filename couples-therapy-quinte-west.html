Couples Therapy in Quinte West & Trenton

Virtual couples therapy for Quinte West, Trenton, and the CFB Trenton community, with in-person sessions nearby in Belleville. With Matt Harris, Registered Psychotherapist.

Trenton · Frankford · Batawa Virtual · In-Person in Belleville Registered Psychotherapist

Military life is hard on relationships in ways that civilian couples rarely have to think about. Deployments and long absences, postings that uproot a partner’s career and community every few years, the strain of reconnecting after time apart, and the quiet load carried by the spouse who keeps everything running at home — all of it can wear down even a strong partnership. In Quinte West, with 8 Wing / CFB Trenton at its centre, these pressures are part of everyday life for many couples.

Hope & Love offers couples therapy that understands that reality, delivered by secure video across Quinte West, with in-person sessions available nearby in Belleville.

What couples come in for

  • Reconnecting after deployment, training, or time apart
  • The strain of frequent postings and relocation on a relationship
  • Communication that has narrowed under stress, or turned into conflict
  • Rebuilding trust after betrayal, secrecy, or addiction

How the work is done

Matt is trained in Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), which helps couples step out of blame-and-withdrawal cycles and understand what each partner is really reaching for underneath the stress. Sessions are balanced and non-blaming, and virtual scheduling can flex around service and shift schedules.

Virtual, with in-person nearby in Belleville

Sessions are held by secure video, which suits the unpredictable timing of service life — and works even when one partner is posted or deployed elsewhere. If you would rather meet in person, Belleville is one of Hope & Love’s two in-person locations, a short drive from Trenton.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do you work with military couples and CFB Trenton families?
Yes. Hope & Love supports couples navigating deployment, relocation, and reintegration. Sessions are held by secure video, with in-person appointments available nearby in Belleville. For service-related concerns, support is also available through the base and the Military Family Resource Centre.
Can we attend if one of us is posted or deployed elsewhere?
Yes. Virtual couples therapy allows both partners to join from different locations, which is often necessary during postings, training, or deployment. Sessions can be scheduled around service commitments.
Does insurance cover couples therapy?
Many Ontario extended health benefit plans cover sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist. Matt provides detailed receipts for insurance submission. Check your plan for "psychotherapy" or "registered psychotherapist" coverage.
