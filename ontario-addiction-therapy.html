Addiction Therapy in Ontario

Hope & Love serves individuals and couples across Ontario, in-person in the GTA and Halton region, and virtually to every corner of the province.

Serving all of Ontario In-person in GTA & Halton Virtual province-wide Registered Psychotherapist

Access to high-quality addiction therapy in Ontario should not depend on where you live. Hope & Love is committed to serving individuals and couples across the province, with in-person sessions available in the Greater Toronto Area and Halton region, and secure virtual sessions available to anyone in Ontario with an internet connection.

In-person sessions

In-person addiction therapy is available in the following areas:

  • Toronto, GTA and surrounding communities
  • Oakville, and Halton region including Burlington and Mississauga

Virtual sessions, anywhere in Ontario

Secure, PHIPA-compliant video sessions are available to anyone in Ontario. Virtual therapy is particularly well-suited for:

  • Individuals in communities without local addiction therapy access
  • People who prefer the privacy of their own home
  • Those dealing with sensitive topics such as pornography or sex addiction
  • Couples who find scheduling in-person sessions difficult
  • People in rural or northern Ontario

What we treat

Hope & Love offers specialized addiction therapy across Ontario for:

  • Pornography addiction
  • Sex addiction and compulsive sexual behaviour
  • Gambling addiction
  • Drug use and substance addiction
  • Alcohol use disorder
  • Technology and screen addiction
  • Couples therapy for addiction

Access and insurance

Sessions are provided by Matt Harris, a Registered Psychotherapist (RP). Many Ontario extended health benefit plans cover registered psychotherapy, check your plan for "psychotherapy" or "registered psychotherapist" coverage. Receipts are provided for all sessions for insurance submission.
