Frequently asked questions

Do you have an office in Napanee?
Sessions for Napanee are held by secure video, with in-person appointments available nearby in Belleville, one of our two in-person locations. Many Napanee clients prefer virtual sessions for the added privacy and convenience.
How quickly can I start therapy in Napanee?
Virtual therapy often means you can begin sooner than local waitlists allow. The first step is a free 20-minute consultation, which you can book online at a time that works for you.
Does insurance cover therapy in Napanee?
Many Ontario extended health benefit plans cover sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist. Matt provides detailed receipts for insurance submission. Check your plan for "psychotherapy" or "registered psychotherapist" coverage.
Which communities near Napanee do you serve?
Virtual therapy is available across Greater Napanee, Newburgh, Deseronto, and the surrounding Lennox & Addington townships, and anywhere in Ontario. In-person sessions are offered in Belleville and Prince Edward County.
