Therapy & Counselling in Quinte West & Trenton

Secure virtual psychotherapy for Quinte West, Trenton, Frankford, and the CFB Trenton community, with in-person sessions a short drive away in Belleville. With Matt Harris, Registered Psychotherapist.

Trenton · Frankford · Batawa Virtual · In-Person in Belleville Registered Psychotherapist

Quinte West is a community shaped in large part by 8 Wing / CFB Trenton, the largest air force base in Canada. That brings a particular set of pressures: deployments and long absences, frequent postings and relocations, the strain of reintegration, and the quiet weight carried by spouses, partners, and children who hold things together at home. Alongside the military community, Trenton, Frankford, and Batawa are home to shift workers, commuters, and families navigating everyday life.

Hope & Love offers therapy that fits around that reality. Secure video sessions are available throughout Quinte West, and because Belleville is one of our in-person locations just up the highway, face-to-face appointments are close by when you want them.

  • Trauma & PTSD therapy in Quinte West — trauma-informed support for the military and first-responder community
  • Anxiety therapy and depression therapy
  • Anger management and addiction therapy

Virtual therapy, with in-person nearby in Belleville

Quinte West is served primarily by secure video, which suits the unpredictable schedules of shift work and service life. If you would rather meet in person, Belleville is one of Hope & Love’s two in-person locations and only a short drive from Trenton. You can mix in-person and virtual sessions as your schedule allows.

Support that understands service life

You do not have to explain the whole culture of military life before you can be understood. Therapy here makes room for the specific pressures of postings, absences, and reintegration, without pathologizing the way you have coped. For service-related injuries, serving members can also access care through the base and Veterans Affairs Canada — and you are welcome here for the parts of life that sit alongside all of that.

Frequently asked questions

Do you have an office in Quinte West or Trenton?
Sessions for Quinte West and Trenton are held by secure video, with in-person appointments available a short drive away in Belleville, one of our two in-person locations. You can combine virtual and in-person sessions as your schedule allows.
Do you work with military families and the CFB Trenton community?
Yes. Hope & Love supports military families, partners, and first responders with the pressures of deployment, relocation, and reintegration. For service-related injuries, serving members can also access care through the base and Veterans Affairs Canada.
Does insurance cover therapy in Quinte West?
Many Ontario extended health benefit plans cover sessions with a Registered Psychotherapist. Matt provides detailed receipts for insurance submission. Check your plan for "psychotherapy" or "registered psychotherapist" coverage.
Can virtual sessions work around shift schedules?
Yes. Secure video sessions are flexible and are often the most practical option for shift workers and service members. Appointment times can be arranged to fit rotating schedules.
